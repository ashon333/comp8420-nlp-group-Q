COMP8420 - Congo Webshop: AI-Powered E-Commerce Intelligence

This E-commerce Intelligent System showcases the integration of modern natural language processing, semantic search, and agentic design. By combining basic NLP techniques—such as text preprocessing, named entity extraction, and TF-IDF recommendations—with advanced LLM features including ChromaDB-backed RAG, aspect-based sentiment parsing, and Chain-of-Thought fake review detection, our team has built a premium, full-stack product portal demonstrating how AI elevates contemporary e-commerce shopping and merchant analytics.
